How I got here

I began exploring design thinking during my BA studies. Over time, I've gained diverse experiences in different design areas and roles within multidisciplinary teams. Interacting with clients from various backgrounds has deepened my understanding of user needs, guiding my strategic design approach. These experiences have shaped me into a flexible and dedicated design strategist, focused on creating impactful solutions that prioritise user satisfaction.

On the side: Learning & creating

In my creative journey, I indulge in a variety of artistic pursuits. From sketching and digital art to the tactile experience of pottery, each medium offers me a unique avenue for self-expression. Whether I'm sketching ideas on paper, bringing them to life digitally, or shaping clay into tangible forms, I find endless inspiration in the process of creation. My passion for art ignites my drive to continuously explore new techniques and push the boundaries of my creativity.
